What is a locally owned business?
At Calico Eats, we define a locally owned business as a privately owned
corporation that has 3 or less locations within a certain area.

What We Do:
Calico Eats is a San Francisco Bay Area-based website that aims to promote
locally owned Bay Area restaurants and eateries by featuring them.
Currently, we only have a limited number of restaurants listed, and
they are organized by area and by category (found in the explore page).

Who We Are:
Our team was created on July 24, 2017 through the Girls Who Code summer
immersion program located at Intuit in Mountain View, California.
We created this website for our Girls Who Code final project. We chose this
idea because we wanted to support our locally owned businesses in the Bay Area.
